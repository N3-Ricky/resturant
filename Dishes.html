Chairman & Yip

Claypot mud crab with sticky rice

"Inspired from a Tainan dish 紅蟳米糕 'steamed crab rice cake', you can also find the dish in cantonese region. We first stir fry mud crab with ginger shallot and sticky rice separately (sticky rice cooked with a supreme chicken stock, similar to paella but done in a wok stir fry) , then we combine them and cook in a claypot together, it allows the rice to absorb all the crab juice and crab roe. The al dente texture of the rice creates contrast between the crab meat."
